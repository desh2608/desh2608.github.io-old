About

I am a graduate student at Johns Hopkins University, working in the Center for Language and Speech Processing (CLSP), advised by Daniel Povey and Sanjeev Khudanpur. My research interests are in theoretical and applied machine learning, particularly unsupervised learning techniques for language and speech.

I graduated from IIT Guwahati in 2017 with a major in Computer Science. My bachelor thesis was on deep learning methods for relation extraction in clinical text, supervised by Prof. Ashish Anand.

When I’m not doing ML, I like to work out, swim, play the guitar, and read fiction.
